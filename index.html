Malcolm X

May 19, 1925 - February 21, 1965

Omaha, Nebraska

  • He opposed integration and advocated for "by any means necessary", which placed him on opposite ends with MLK.
  • In 1964, he founded Afro-American Unity, which focused on racism as the enemy rather than the white race.
  • He went to prison for 6.5 years for stealing a watch, where he later changed his name to Malcoln X and converted to the Nation of Islam.

“You get freedom by letting your enemy know that you'll do anything to get your freedom; then you'll get it. It's the only way you'll get it.”
